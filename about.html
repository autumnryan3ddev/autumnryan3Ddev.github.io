About

Autumn Ryan is a 3D artist specializing in stylized, whimsical characters and props. Throughout her education, she has: 

  • acted as the lead 3D artist for her capstone project Re:Silica, building valuable teamworking experience alongside 3D development skills
  • contributed to game projects in a variety of roles, including writing, programming, sound design, and UI art, leading to a broad understanding of the game development pipeline and good inter-team communication 
  • actively participated in the G-Nonymous game development club while studying abroad at George Mason Korea, fostering to her ability to work well on an international team

Autumn is currently studying at George Mason University and expects to graduate with a BFA in Computer Game Design in May 2026.
